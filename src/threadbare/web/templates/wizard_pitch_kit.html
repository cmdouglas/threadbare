What Threadbare does

Hand this to the rest of your mod team.

  • Threadbare mirrors public (@everyone-readable) channels into a searchable, paginated forum view. It's read-only -- it never posts, reacts, or writes back to Discord.
  • Deletions on Discord are removed from the mirror within seconds via the gateway, and a nightly sweep catches anything missed during an outage.
  • Stored per message: content, author display name/avatar, timestamps, attachments (proxied on demand, not mirrored to local storage), and aggregate reaction counts. No per-user reaction identity, no emails, no presence data.
  • Only channels you explicitly confirm as indexed are mirrored -- and if a channel's permissions change so @everyone can no longer read it, its content is automatically removed from the index.
  • There are no backups of mirrored message content. Discord remains the sole source of truth; the mirror can always be rebuilt from scratch.
  • Any member of the server may log in and read. Per-channel indexing and sync health are controlled from the admin page, available to anyone with Manage Server or Administrator on the server.
